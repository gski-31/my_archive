QR Codes have been around since the 90's, but they have only recently become popular in the U.S. market.

 
  So, just what is a QR Code?  
 
A QR, or Quick Response Code, is a specifically generated 2D barcode that stores over 7,000 characters, and can be read or decoded with any web enabled Smartphone. Today's Smartphones allow you to download a QR Code reader, which allows a Smartphone to scan the code and read the information.

 
  What is a QR Campaign?  
 
QRGameplan's technology based tools and knowledge help clients maximize their marketing efforts and dollars by gathering data and tracking web traffic. We work individually and confidentially with each client to help set up specific, targeted campaigns so that our clients use their marketing dollars effectively and with impact. Why run an ad in the newspaper if you don't know how many people are actually looking at it? QRGameplan helps you track and capture data that shows if you are spending your time and money wisely.
