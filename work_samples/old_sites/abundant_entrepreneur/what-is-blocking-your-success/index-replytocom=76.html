So no matter which life-area is blocked (love-life, money, success, weight, etc.), we can always look for the root of it in one of the 5 Most Common Types of Blocks. Of the top five most common types of blocks, which seem most familiar?

#2 Culture and Religion Blocks:

It‚Äôs no surprise really that our cultures, and our religions have a huge influence on our belief systems around money.

  • ‚ÄúMoney is the root of all evil‚Äù

The third thing that can block someone‚Äôs success is a low self-esteem or lack of confidence… an underlying feeling that they’re not good enough, and that everything is personal.

These people feel as if OTHER people can do it – but they can’t. They lack the belief in themselves and so question their own ability to handle success. From this place, taking any action becomes a struggle because it becomes an immediate war with their fear of failing.

Those with healthy self-esteem recognize that ‚Äòtaking action‚Äô is a Learning Opportunity – an experiment to try something and see what works and what doesn‚Äôt. #4 “Inability to Receive” Blocks

The fourth way that I find people blocking their own success is by not allowing themselves to Receive..

Some people find it easy to Give, but haven’t yet developed the quality that allows OTHERS to give to THEM.

We need to be a giver AND a receiver – We must allow the FLOW in both ways! Not being able to receive success is a huge block!

It‚Äôs tough when we‚Äôve been taught that “all the joy is in the giving” and that “it is better to give than to receive.” But if everyone is GIVING all the time, then there is no one left to give to! In that way, not receiving can actually be selfish.

#5 “Conscious Mind Blindness” Block

The final form of Success Blocks is a general sense of blindness to the opportunities and invitations around us – opportunities that would open the door to more success and wealth!

You see, our subconscious mind only feeds our conscious thoughts a small percentage of what it is taking in (about 5% at the most).

Basically, what it has been taught is of interest or importance to us. And after years of success-blocking, our habits have trained the subconscious mind to only let us see the things that will continue to keep us unsuccessful,

Really think about this – If up until this point in life, the attention has been mainly noticing all the areas where we were NOT successful, our subconscious mind thinks THAT’S what we’re most interested in. It’s almost like losing the ability to see opportunities!

By our reactions and thoughts, we have trained our subconscious mind that we aren’t interested in those things… they aren’t relevant to us… we can’t take advantage of them. So our conscious mind becomes blinded to the opportunities to make money that are right there in front of us! Instead, it gathers evidence of even more things that we CAN’T do and ways that we DON’T measure up.

As we start living life as a more successful person — and our decisions, emotions, actions, and behavior are more aligned with those of a more wealthy, successful person – it becomes clear that there have been so many opportunities around us the whole time!
